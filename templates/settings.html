FAQ


- Journal Express is an anonymous posting platform to allow your thoughts, feelings, ideas, and written forms of art to be seen by other writting enthusiasts all around the world!

- The goal of Journal Express was to create "A diary accessible by everyone", which might sound strange compared to the openness of the platform compared to the secrecy of a diary. To keep to a traditional concept of a diary, I wanted to allow comfort on by letting posts be a brain dump without fear of judgement.

- Likes on the internet can be described as an individual's digital currency. One of my goals was to remove a feeling of external validation, which may lead to holding back of creative endeavors and I want out users to feel free to dedicate making their profiles into their own open-access journal
